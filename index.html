Visit Wilkes-Barre

Enjoy culture and sports in this historical city that boasts an electic nightlife.

Top 3 Activities to do in Wilkes-Barre

Attend a concert

Enjoy some great wine and even better music at a place the locals love to go.

Check out a hockey game

Have a beer or two while experiencing an exciting hockey match courtesy of the Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Penguins.

Enjoy a performance or a show

Take in a theater performance or other cultural event at the iconic F.M. Kirby Center.
